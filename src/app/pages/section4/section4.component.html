Key Metrics to Measure Digital Learning Scale & Engagement in FY’21 - Adoption, Active Learners & Return Visits

SCALE

L&D set a goal of achieving 75% adoption, 15% Active Learners and a Return Rate of 2 times per month. All goals were achieved by end of FY’21.


  • Adoption: Number of banded employees with a profile in My Learning




  • Return Visits: Number of logins per month

ENGAGEMENT

  • Active Learners: Number of employees with a profile who have shared 1 item, followed 3 skills, marked 5 items complete and logs in at least once every 30 days.
